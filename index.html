about me

A Nashville “Native,” I’ve had my fair share of different jobs and even career paths: from being a Marketing student (and graduate) in college, through 10 years as a graphic designer, to a high end photography retoucher… Now I’m headed back to school again at the Nashville Software School, and I’m very excited to see where I’ll be come 2017.

summary

I have thirteen years field experience with competencies from print design to prepress to photo retouching. Print design experience includes logos, posters, retail products and displays, business cards, newspaper advertisements, vehicle wraps, store fronts and other promotional pieces. Two years of prepress experience with layout, postscript troubleshooting, and client education/assistance. Photography experience include fine art, portrait and product photography, retouching, compositing, color correction, and general editing skills.

I am comfortable in both PC and Mac environments. Proficient in Photoshop, QuarkXPress and Microsoft Office Suite. Familiar with CorelDraw, Illustrator, Acrobat, and InDesign. Willing and able to learn new programs, techniques, trends and systems.

Specialties: Concept development and actualization; creative writing, photography, and design; mock-up production and assembly; pre-press; photography; brainstorming.
